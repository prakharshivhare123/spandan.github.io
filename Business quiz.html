Guidelines

  • The team has to compete throughout the tournament with the same four players.

  • The team captain has to confirm their participation.

  • All the team members are required to be online and join the match rooms on time.

  • The players need to ensure that their team takes a screenshot of the kill sheet and sends it to us.

  • On the first day, three classic matches will be played among the teams. Meanwhile, on the second day, one classic match and round 2 will be played.

  • In case of an emergency, a substitute may be added before the tournament, but only after taking permission from the organizers.

  • The Teams have to report 10 minutes before the allotted time.

  • The maps for the four classic matches are Erangel, Sanhok, Erangel, and Sanhok, respectively, for the rounds.

  • The Maps for TDM Rounds is – Warehouse.

  • The ranking of the teams would be accordingly to the Total Points.

  • In the case of a tie in total points between the teams for a position:

  • The first preference will be awarded to the WWCD column;

  • The second preference will be given to Kill Point; and,

  • The third preference would be given to placement points.

  • Racism or acts of toxicity and abuse would lead to the disqualification of the whole team.
